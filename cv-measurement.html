About the Product

AThis system leverages industrial cameras and computer vision techniques to measure dimensions with high precision in real-time. By automating manual inspections, the solution enhances efficiency, accuracy, and consistency in quality assurance processes.

Motivation

The motivation behind this project was to enhance dimensional measurement, replacing traditional inspection tools and gauges, whike ensuring efficient functioning and user accesibility.
