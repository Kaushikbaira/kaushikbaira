Computer Vision-Based Dimensional Measurement System

Developed an advanced system for real-time automated dimensional quality inspections using computer vision.

Organization: Alcom Extrusions Pvt Ltd
Role: Lead Developer

About the Product

This system leverages industrial cameras and computer vision techniques to measure dimensions with high precision in real-time. By automating manual inspections, the solution enhances efficiency, accuracy, and consistency in quality assurance processes.

Motivation

The motivation behind this project was to streamline manufacturing quality inspection processes. By replacing manual measurement methods with a computer vision-based system, we aimed to reduce inspection time and eliminate human errors, ensuring adherence to critical tolerances.
